Frequently Asked Questions

How does Duplicate Guard detect duplicates?
We analyze every incoming order and compare it with your recent order history based on customer email, phone number, and shipping address. If we find a match within your specified time window, we flag it.
Does it work on the free plan?
Yes! The free plan includes full duplicate detection for up to 30 duplicates per month. It's perfect for testing the app or for stores with lower volume.
What happens when an order is flagged?
We automatically add a tag (e.g., Merge_Review_Candidate) to the order in Shopify. This allows you to filter orders in your admin or set up automation flows to hold fulfillment.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from the app dashboard. There are no long-term contracts.
Why can't I just use Shopify Flow for this?
Shopify Flow is powerful, but it wasn't built specifically for duplicate detection. It often ignores SKU-specific filters or fires incorrectly, leading to false positives or missed duplicates. Duplicate Guard was built specifically to fill this gap with precision tracking across 24-hour windows.
Does it work for COD (Cash-on-Delivery) stores without customer emails?
Yes! Duplicate Guard is perfect for COD and phone-first checkout flows common in markets like India. We detect duplicates using phone number + name or phone number + address matching—no email required. If your checkout only collects mobile numbers, we've got you covered.
